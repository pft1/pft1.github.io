Crew

Hi, my name is Federico. Very young I started sailing with my father on his 1930 wooden Yawl. At fourteen I was sent in Isle of Wight for a private sailing course and since then sailing is been my passion. In the last 10 years I turned my passion into my work, and I have chartered different sailing vessels for companies and private clients in the Mediterranean and in open sea. I got my Yacht-master certificate in 2020 along with my Italian sailing license.
